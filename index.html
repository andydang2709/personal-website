About Me

Hi, I'm Andy Dang, a dual-degree student at Emory University's Goizueta Business School, pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics and a Bachelor of Business Administration.

My expertise lies in data analytics, strategy, and technology, with hands-on experience in programming (Python, R, SQL, Shell) and utilizing tools such as AWS, Azure, Power BI, and Tableau.

I have held multiple positions and internships in a startup, advertising agency, and IT company. Moreover, I am an incoming Business Intelligence Engineer Intern at Amazon. Additionally, I have won multiple case competitions such as the 2024 Purdue Data4Good Case Competition and the 2023 Emory PwC Case Competition.

Outside of my professional world, I have a strong passion for cooking, jazz, barista, mixology, and badminton.
